Save Our Street Ministries

Save Our Street Ministries Service is a haven for the community. For children it offers bible studies, dinner and aims to provide a safe breath to children from torn homes. They provide rehab, bible studies, and vocational skills for men. They teach plumbing, welding, teamwork, and how to manage finances. For woman, they offer a bible studies, emotional support and a retreat to relax and get closer to your community.

My organization works with Save Our Street Ministries after school care. We serve as leaders teaching small lessons and helping with basic math and other forms of homework. For younger groups, we will lead in a game and just keep children entertained until their parents return from Bible study or other event.

I really enjoy build as it allows you to meet a new very friendly family and get closer to the guys we work with.
